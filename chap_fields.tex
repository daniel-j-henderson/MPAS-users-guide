\chapter{Description of Model Fields}
Every field that may be read or written in a NetCDF {\em stream} (as described in Chapter \ref{chap:mpas_io}) by the MPAS-Atmosphere model is described in this chapter. The dimensionality of each field is given in Fortran storage order (i.e., the fastest-varying dimension is inner-most).
